"ALL YOU CAN TASTE CONCEPT"



Just seat at your table and order your favourite drink: our friendly waiters will walkk through the tables offering different slices of pizza: you can accept the flavour or refuse it, waiting for the next one.
The formula is all you can taste as much as you want with no time limits.

We have a lot of vegetarian or "no pork" recipes!

Once you are completely fulfilled just communicate it to us by turning your able number cube from the green to the red side.
Each delicious combination of fresh Italian ingredients is coming hot straight from the kitchen!
